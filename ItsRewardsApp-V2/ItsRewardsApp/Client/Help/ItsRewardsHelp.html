Getting Started

Before you can sign in you must register your account with the ItsRewards App

Register your account using email and cell phone number



After registering your account you can go to the home screen of ItsRewards and sign in using either your email or phone number that you entered when creating your account



The first step after you log in is to choose which store you want to access discounts for. Some people may belong to multiple loyalty programs for multiple stores, this is where you would be able to narrow down which store you are travelling to.

After you select the proper store, you can then choose either Coupons or Offers

Coupons

Tobacco coupons available to you for that store

Offers

Tobacco specific coupons available to you for that store
